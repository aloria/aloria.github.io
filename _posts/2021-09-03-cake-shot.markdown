Create a party in your mouth with the mysteries of mixology. <br/><br/>
## What you need:
* 1 oz Frangelico
* 0.5 oz vodka
* A wedge of lemon
* A shot glass
<br/>
<br/>

## Do This:
* Combine the vodka and Frangelico in a shot glass
* Dip the lemon wedge into sugar
* Suck the lemon wedge and take the shot
* Drink responsibly.
